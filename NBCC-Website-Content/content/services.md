Visiting a new church can be intimidating if you don’t know what to expect. As soon as you enter the doors of NBCC, you will be welcomed by a diverse group of believers. We sing, pray and worship together. We have services for all ages and even our youngest devotees are catered to with our youth activities. Come and pray with us.
