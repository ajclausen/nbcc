John Clausen serves as an Elder and Interim Minister at NBCC. He provides spiritual leadership and guidance to our congregation during this transitional time.

John is committed to establishing Christ's Church in our community according to the pattern left in the New Testament by Christ and His Apostles, working to restore the church in doctrine, ordinances, and life.
